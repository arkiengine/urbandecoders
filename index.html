01 / About

Technology for Tomorrow's Cities

Urban Decoders is an AEC technology company developing and providing emerging technologies for urban design and architecture. We focus on Digital Twins, artificial intelligence, and game-engine technologies — and how these tools will shape the future of cities.

Founded by Michal Gryko, a chartered architect (RIBA/ARB) and Design & Urban Decoders bridges the gap between architectural practice and cutting-edge technology.

Through our professional services, regular YouTube tutorials, and blog posts, we aim to educate and inform the public about the potential of these technologies and how they can be used to create better, smarter, more sustainable cities.

Core Digital Twins for masterplanning & urban design
AI ComfyUI, LLM fine-tuning, LoRA & generative design
Engine Unreal Engine 5 & real-time visualisation
Data GIS pipelines, FME & spatial data analytics
Product Arki Engine — flagship design platform
Content YouTube tutorials & educational resources
02 / Flagship Product

Arki Engine

Arki Engine is the flagship product of Urban Decoders' research and development. An Unreal Engine-powered, real-time design platform that brings architectural and spatial ideas to life through advanced data-driven workflows, configurable design systems, and complex geometric and analytical computation.

All wrapped in a high-fidelity visualisation environment that streamlines material choices, configuration options, and overall design efficiency — from parametric pavilions to giga-scale masterplans.
